Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

SHS Game Hall ("we," "our," or "us") collects information to provide and improve our services:

  • Profile Information: When you create a profile, we collect your username and optional profile picture.
  • Usage Data: We track game clicks, visitor counts, and interaction with our features to improve user experience.
  • Chat Messages: Messages you send in our community chat are stored to facilitate communication.
  • Cookies and Local Storage: We use browser local storage to remember your preferences and profile information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our gaming platform
  • To enable social features like chat, friends, and leaderboards
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our services
  • To display personalized content and recommendations

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: We display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can learn more about how Google uses data at Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase: We use Google Firebase for data storage and real-time features. Firebase's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and local storage to:

  • Remember your profile and preferences
  • Track visitor statistics
  • Enable personalized features

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your profile information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of certain data collection through browser settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our page.

About SHS Game Hall

Our Mission

SHS Game Hall is a comprehensive gaming platform designed to provide students and gaming enthusiasts with access to high-quality unblocked games, interactive community features, and a safe, engaging online environment.

What We Offer

  • Extensive Game Library: Browse and play hundreds of carefully curated games across multiple categories including action, puzzle, strategy, and more.
  • Community Features: Connect with other players through our chat system, friends list, leaderboards, and social polls.
  • Interactive Canvas: Express your creativity on our shared drawing canvas.
  • User Profiles: Create and customize your profile with a unique username and profile picture.
  • Real-Time Updates: Experience live updates across all features, from game statistics to chat messages.

Our Values

  • Accessibility: We believe gaming should be accessible to everyone, regardless of network restrictions.
  • Community: We foster a positive, inclusive community where players can connect and share experiences.
  • Quality: We curate our game selection to ensure only the best, most engaging games are featured.
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our platform with new features and enhancements.

How It Works

Simply browse our game collection, click on any game cube to start playing, and explore our social features to connect with the community. All games are embedded directly on our platform for seamless access.

Technology

SHS Game Hall is built using modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and Firebase for real-time data synchronization. We prioritize performance, security, and user experience in everything we build.
