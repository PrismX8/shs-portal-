Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

What we collect

  • Chat & reactions: Messages, reactions, typing state, and usernames you post in global chat (stored in Supabase with timestamps and an optional state/region label).
  • Location (optional): If you allow it, we request device location to show a general state/region tag in chat. If blocked, we try an IP-based region lookup instead. Precise coordinates are not stored.
  • Usage & performance: Basic event logs for visitor counts, popular games, and feature usage to keep the site fast, especially on low-end devices.
  • Ads & analytics: Google AdSense (ads/personalization cookies) and Microsoft Clarity (session analytics). Visitor counters run via OptiStats and FreeVisitorCounters.
  • Local storage only on your device: Theme, chat name/color, bubble theme, last detected state, recent games/favorites, unread counts, and other in-site preferences.

How we use it

  • Deliver real-time chat, reactions, and typing indicators.
  • Remember your preferences and keep performance smooth.
  • Measure what content works (aggregated stats) and fund the site with ads.

Sharing

Data goes only to the services above (Supabase for chat, AdSense for ads, Clarity/visitor counters for analytics). We do not sell personal data.

Your choices

  • Decline location prompts or revoke them in your browser/device settings.
  • Clear site data/local storage anytime to remove on-device preferences.
  • Use ad settings to control AdSense personalization.
  • Contact us to request removal of specific chat messages you posted.

Security & retention

We secure chat and settings data with Supabase and HTTPS. Chat history and reactions are retained while the feature exists; local storage stays only on your device until you clear it.

Kids

Nebulo is a general-audience site. Children should avoid sharing personal details in chat.

About Nebulo

Our Mission

Nebulo is a comprehensive gaming platform designed to provide students and gaming enthusiasts with access to high-quality education games, interactive community features, and a safe, engaging online environment.

What We Offer

  • Extensive Game Library: Browse and play hundreds of carefully curated games across multiple categories including action, puzzle, strategy, and more.
  • Community Features: Connect with other players through our chat system, friends list, leaderboards, and social polls.
  • Interactive Canvas: Express your creativity on our shared drawing canvas.
  • User Profiles: Create and customize your profile with a unique username and profile picture.
  • Real-Time Updates: Experience live updates across all features, from game statistics to chat messages.

Our Values

  • Accessibility: We believe gaming should be accessible to everyone, regardless of network restrictions.
  • Community: We foster a positive, inclusive community where players can connect and share experiences.
  • Quality: We curate our game selection to ensure only the best, most engaging games are featured.
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our platform with new features and enhancements.

How It Works

Simply browse our game collection, click on any game cube to start playing, and explore our social features to connect with the community. All games are embedded directly on our platform for seamless access.

Technology

Nebulo is built using modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and Firebase for real-time data synchronization. We prioritize performance, security, and user experience in everything we build.
