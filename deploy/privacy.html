Privacy Policy

Last updated: Dec 19, 2025

1. What we collect

  • Chat username you choose (stored in your browser and sent with chat messages).
  • Chat messages you send (stored in our database).
  • Voice presence metadata (your chosen name + a temporary peer id) while you are in voice.
  • Local settings stored in your browser (e.g., theme, voice consent gate, local mutes).

2. Voice audio

Voice audio is sent peer-to-peer between users (WebRTC). We do not intentionally store your live voice audio on the server. However, other users may record audio on their devices.

3. How we use information

  • Operate chat and voice features
  • Safety and moderation (reports, timeouts, bans)
  • Improve reliability and fix bugs

4. Data sharing

We share data with our infrastructure providers necessary to run the Service (e.g., realtime/database providers). We do not sell your personal information.

5. Your choices

  • You can stop using voice chat at any time by leaving the call.
  • You can clear local browser storage to remove saved settings on your device.

6. Contact

If you have questions, contact the site owner.
